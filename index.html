Amaury Baret

PhD candidate in condensed-matter physics, Department of Physics, University of Liège.

I study stochastic media and the physics of energy-management surfaces — in particular, transport and optical response in disordered metallic nanowire networks used for spectrally selective coatings and radiative cooling. My work pairs theoretical models of percolation and Mie scattering with large-scale numerical simulation on high-performance clusters.
